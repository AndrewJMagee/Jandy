Abide - Cambridge, MA

Where the boba is tasty and the atmosphere open

October, 2019 Jeff

If you are around Central Square in Cambridge MA -maybe taking a tour of Harvard or MIT-, you should stop by the small boba shop called Abide. The owners are frequently present and always pleasant. As New England is plagued by big chains when it comes to boba, abide acts as an oasis. The atmosphere is wonderful, warm, hip, and very open -at night it is converted to a dance club- and there is always something entertaining being projected on there large screen. Overall, it is a nice place to sit down for a while, have some boba and get some work done.
