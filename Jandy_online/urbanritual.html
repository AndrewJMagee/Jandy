Urban Ritual - San Francisco

The reigning champion in the hearts of the Jandy authors

July, 2019 Andy

Despite our efforts, the Jandy team has not yet found a boba as delicious as Urban Ritual. When you are in San Francisco -a hub for amazing boba- you may be overwhelmed by Boba Guys and the likes, but if possible you must make it to Fell St in Hayes Valley. The staff is absolutely kind and helpful, the samples are delicious and the tea is of extreme quality. The boba, always fresh has an a perfect taste and texture.
